WHAT IS THINK GLOBAL?

Think Global is a service that offers international exchange programs to and from all over the world. The types of exchanges offered by us are paid internships, studying abroad, working abroad and volunteering all over the globe.

At Think Global, we will connect University students in their second or third year to opportunities that could further their career in the future. Take your pick of what we have to offer!

OUR VISION

Here at Think Global we envision a world where everyone is respected and accepted, no matter what their beliefs, identity or way of life is. Our core value is to appreciate the cultural diversity that exists in the world.

We provide exchange programs that will contribute to the understand and exploration of different cultures in different societies, with various religious, politcal, and economic systems.
